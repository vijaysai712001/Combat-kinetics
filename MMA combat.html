WHAT WE DO IN THIS CLASS:

Kinetics 101 is designed to be the essential entry/re-entry point for any combat sport to provide athletes with a solid foundation of technique on which his/her sport specific training can be built.

As with any elite level athletic program, jumping right in without proper prep and conditioning or improper form can result in injury or bad motor skills. The Kinetics 101 segment serves as a foundational stone in one’s MMA journey and uses the latest advances in sport science fused with traditional martial arts methodology to develop refined motor skills, stable joints and strong muscles.

This program is a stepping stone into the world of MMA and will build a “Martial Artist’s” agility, balance, coordination, flexibility, explosive power, endurance and focus across the board.
