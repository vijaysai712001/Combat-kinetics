CK TEYNAPET

Semi-Indoor MMA Club Chennai
 FUNCTIONAL FITNESS, PERFORMANCE FITNESS, MMA, BOXING, KICKBOXING, MUAY THAI, SUBMISSION WRESTLING.

 With an air of resilience, one of our oldest existing branches… compact, clean and functional, the Teynampet MMA club is a space with a bifurcated striking and grappling area. Equipped with all necessary equipment including heavy bags and multi-level pull up bars, it is a perfect place for students to experience some #madskills.
 Located atop the HDFC Bank building in Cenotaph Road, our Teynampet MMA club is always bustling with enthusiastic and driven students eager to push their boundaries with focused training. The programs offered include Core Combat MMA, Striking and Kinetics 101, which are sure to get your engines revving.
